Fryer’s Delight Takeaway – Best Pizza

Edinburgh

In the competitive world of Edinburgh takeaways, Fryer’s Delight Takeaway has carved out a name for itself with a focus on flavour, freshness and unbeatable value. This year, their hard work and commitment have earned them the Best Pizza title at the Mealzo for Business Awards.

Led by Tanweer Nazir Qureshi, the team at Fryer’s Delight Takeaway has built their reputation on consistently delivering delicious pizzas, made fresh to order and loaded with quality toppings. From classic margheritas to bold meat feasts and spicy veggie options, there’s something on the menu for every taste.

What sets them apart is not just their food, but the way they treat their customers. Their customer reviews highlight friendly service, quick delivery and pizzas that hit the spot every time. Many regulars have dubbed it their “go-to pizza place” in Edinburgh, and the award simply confirms what locals already knew.

“We’re truly grateful to Mealzo and to our loyal customers. This award means the world to us and reflects the effort the whole team puts in, day in and day out.”

Thanks to their partnership with Mealzo, Fryer’s Delight Takeaway has seen a steady rise in online orders and customer reach across the Edinburgh area. The recognition not only celebrates their craft but also marks a strong chapter of growth in their journey.
