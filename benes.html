Bene’s – Best Takeaway

East Lothian

In East Lothian, one name continues to stand out on the takeaway scene. Bene’s has officially taken home the title of Best Takeaway, a recognition earned through consistently positive customer feedback and a dedication to food done right.

Owned by Hussain Omer, Bene’s is a local institution that offers a bit of everything, but with care and attention that sets them apart. Their bestsellers include traditional fish and chips, hearty burgers and pizzas that keep customers coming back.

Whether it is a family tea or a quick bite after work, Bene’s has become a trusted name in the community. Reviewers praise the food quality, generous portions and friendly staff.

“Thanks to Mealzo for helping us grow our customer base and be seen by more people. We really appreciate the support and are proud of this achievement.”

Mealzo’s platform has helped Bene’s expand its reach while staying true to its roots. The award recognises their consistency and popularity with customers throughout East Lothian.

Top seller: Traditional fish and chips
