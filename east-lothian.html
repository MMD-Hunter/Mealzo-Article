Lothian Takeaways Honoured

Outstanding local takeaways across West and East Lothian have been celebrated at this year’s Mealzo for Business Awards, taking home honours in categories that reflect their popularity, service and quality. Chosen based on glowing customer reviews, food excellence and their growth through the Mealzo platform, these award-winning businesses represent the heart of local food culture.

Bene’s – Best Takeaway

East Lothian

In East Lothian, one name continues to stand out on the takeaway scene. Bene’s has officially taken home the title of Best Takeaway, a recognition earned through consistently positive customer feedback and a dedication to food done right. Owned by Hussain Omer, Bene’s is a local institution that offers a bit of everything, but with care and attention that sets them apart.

“Thanks to Mealzo for helping us grow our customer base and be seen by more people. We really appreciate the support and are proud of this achievement.”

Top sellers: Fish & Chips, Burgers, and Pizzas

Joe’s – Hidden Gem Award

East Lothian

Joe’s, a family-run takeaway nestled in East Lothian, has taken home the Hidden Gem Award. Led by Dario Andrucci, Joe’s has always focused on quality ingredients, traditional recipes and genuine service. Though it may not sit on a high street, its loyal following and glowing reviews show that good food speaks for itself.

“It’s an honour to be called a hidden gem. We’re thankful to Mealzo and especially to our customers who continue to support us. This award is for them.”

Top sellers: Freshly Fried Fish, Handmade Pizzas

TKH Fish Bar – Customer Favourite

East Lothian

Known for its crisp batter, golden chips and generous portions, TKH Fish Bar has officially won the Customer Favourite award in East Lothian. Owner Sabir Eroglu puts customer experience at the heart of everything. From the moment an order is placed to the final bite, the team focuses on freshness, quality and speed.

“We’re grateful for this recognition and we want to thank Mealzo and every customer who’s taken the time to leave a review or place an order. It keeps us going.”

Top seller: The Classic Fish Supper
