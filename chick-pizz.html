Chick Pizz – Best Fried Chicken

Denton Burn

Tucked away in the heart of Denton Burn, Chick Pizz has been quietly building a reputation as the area’s go-to spot for proper fried chicken. Run by Stephen Phillip Burgon, Chick Pizz is known for doing things the old-fashioned way. Real ingredients, careful preparation and a team that puts pride into every single box that leaves the shop.

“This win is all down to our amazing customers and our team who put the effort in every day. We are proud to be part of the Mealzo community, and we are absolutely buzzing with the recognition.”
