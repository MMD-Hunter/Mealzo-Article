Four Newcastle Takeaways Recognised

The annual Mealzo for Business Awards celebrate the best of local food businesses, with winners selected based on consistent customer feedback, popular demand, and standout service.

The Mealzo for Business Awards are a tribute to the hard work and quality behind Newcastle’s best independent takeaways. This year, four local favourites earned top honours—each celebrated by their community and now recognised across the city.
Chick Pizz – Best Fried Chicken
Denton Burn
Golden, crisp chicken – Denton Burn’s favourite
Locals in Denton Burn have long favoured Chick Pizz for its exceptional fried chicken—and now the recognition is official. Proprietor Stephen Phillip Burgon expressed his gratitude for the accolade.
This recognition is entirely down to our dedicated customers and hardworking team. We’re proud to be part of the Mealzo community.
Top seller: Fried chicken box
Get Stuffed – Best Kebab
Newcastle upon Tyne
Generous portions, bold flavours – city centre classic
Get Stuffed has officially been recognised as the home of the finest kebab in Newcastle. Managed by Kerim Seven, this city-centre establishment is renowned for generous portions and bold, distinctive flavours.
We never cut corners—only the finest ingredients, careful preparation, and quality service. This award means a lot as it’s driven by what our customers truly favour.
Top seller: Mixed doner kebab
Ritz Fish & Chips – Best Fish & Chips
Columbia, Washington
Classic fish and chips, freshly made – always popular
Ritz Fish & Chips, based in Columbia, has been recognised for excellence, earning the Best Fish & Chips title. Under the stewardship of Manveer Singh, Ritz is celebrated for its flaky cod and properly cooked chips.
Our ethos is to keep it simple and do it well. This recognition truly motivates us and we’re grateful for the continued support of our customers.
Top seller: Traditional fish supper
Sorrento Pizza Ltd – Best Pizza
Birtley
Handcrafted pizzas, fresh ingredients, prompt service
Sorrento Pizza Ltd, located in Birtley, has topped the rankings as Best Pizza. Owner Ali Rowhani and his team focus on using fresh ingredients and prompt service to delight customers.
It means a great deal to be recognised in this way. We’ve worked hard to create something people in our community truly favour, and Mealzo has helped us reach even more of them.
Top seller: Classic pepperoni pizza

Celebrating Local Excellence

These awards honour the dedication, skill, and passion behind every independent food business. From Denton Burn to Birtley, Mealzo proudly champions the finest local talent in the North East’s takeaway scene.

Congratulations to all four recipients—the pride of Newcastle’s food community.
