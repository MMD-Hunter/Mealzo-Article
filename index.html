Four Newcastle Takeaways Crowned Winners at Mealzo for Business Awards

Four much-loved takeaways in the Newcastle upon Tyne area have been officially recognised at this year’s Mealzo for Business Awards, each taking home top honours in their respective categories.

The Mealzo for Business Awards shine a spotlight on the hard-working independent businesses that keep our local food scene thriving. Winners are selected based on outstanding customer reviews, popularity across the Mealzo platform and overall quality of food and service.

This year’s North East winners represent the very best in fried chicken, kebabs, fish and chips and pizza. Their success reflects both their commitment to great food and their strong connection with their local communities.

Here is a closer look at the four champions of 2025:
Chick Pizz – Best Fried Chicken
Denton Burn
Tucked away in the heart of Denton Burn, Chick Pizz has been quietly building a reputation as the area’s go-to spot for proper fried chicken. Now they have been officially recognised with the Best Fried Chicken award, and it is not hard to see why.

Run by Stephen Phillip Burgon, Chick Pizz is known for doing things the old-fashioned way. Real ingredients, careful preparation and a team that puts pride into every single box that leaves the shop.

Their star dish, a perfectly seasoned fried chicken box, has become a local favourite. The chicken is crisp on the outside, tender inside and bursting with flavour that keeps customers coming back. Whether it is a midweek treat or a weekend indulgence, their boxes have become a staple in homes across the area.
“This win is all down to our amazing customers and our team who put the effort in every day. We are proud to be part of the Mealzo community, and we are absolutely buzzing with the recognition.”
Since partnering with Mealzo, Chick Pizz has seen a steady increase in online orders and visibility across Newcastle, helping them grow while staying loyal to their neighbourhood roots.
Top seller: Fried chicken box
Get Stuffed – Best Kebab
Newcastle upon Tyne
In the heart of the city, Get Stuffed has built a loyal following, one flavour-packed wrap at a time. This year they have taken the top spot for Best Kebab, and it is a title they wear proudly.

Owned and operated by Kerim Seven, the takeaway has earned a reputation for generous portions, freshly prepared ingredients and that hard-to-find balance of quality and speed. Their customer reviews reflect what locals already know. Get Stuffed delivers every time.

Their most popular dish is the mixed doner kebab, layered with slow-cooked meats, fresh salad and their famous house sauce. All wrapped in warm naan or served up in a box, ready to go.
“We do not cut corners. It is quality ingredients, proper prep and good service. Winning this means a lot because it is based on what customers think. That is what really matters to us.”
The partnership with Mealzo has opened new doors, helping the shop reach new audiences beyond the city centre while retaining its local charm.
Top seller: Mixed doner kebab
Ritz Fish & Chips – Best Fish & Chips
Columbia, Washington
No British awards list would be complete without a proper chippy. Ritz Fish & Chips, based in Columbia, Washington, has earned their place with pride. Winning the title of Best Fish & Chips, they have proven that tradition, quality and consistency never go out of style.

Owned by Manveer Singh, Ritz has long been a reliable fixture in the local community. From pensioners looking for a Friday night classic to younger customers discovering proper chippy fare, Ritz has managed to appeal across the board.

Their best-selling order is the classic fish supper. Flaky cod, hand-cut chips and mushy peas served just as they should be. Everything is cooked fresh to order and wrapped in familiar paper, bringing that comforting sense of nostalgia with every bite.
“We are all about keeping it simple and doing it well. This award gives us a real boost and we are grateful to our customers for their support and trust over the years.”
Mealzo has helped Ritz connect with new customers across Washington and beyond, particularly those looking for a trusted chippy on a modern platform.
Top seller: Traditional fish supper
Sorrento Pizza – Best Pizza
Birtley
Over in Birtley, Sorrento Pizza has been quietly leading the way in the local pizza scene. Now, with the Best Pizza award firmly in hand, their success is finally getting the spotlight it deserves.

Founded by Ali Rowhani, Sorrento Pizza is built on one core principle. Keep it fresh, and keep it real. That means handmade dough, high-quality toppings and pizzas cooked to perfection in their signature ovens. Whether it is a quick slice or a full family meal, their menu hits the mark.

Their most requested order is the classic pepperoni. Rich tomato sauce, bubbling mozzarella and just the right amount of spice. But beyond the favourites, Sorrento Pizza also offers a wide range of custom options, catering to every type of pizza lover.
“It means a lot to be recognised like this. We have worked hard to build something people love, and Mealzo has helped us reach more of them, especially those who might not have known we were just around the corner.”
Since joining Mealzo, Sorrento Pizza has enjoyed a steady increase in online traffic and customer loyalty, all while maintaining their reputation for quality and consistency.
Top seller: Classic pepperoni

Celebrating Local Excellence

These wins represent more than just good food. They highlight the effort, passion and resilience that define independent food businesses in the North East. From Denton Burn to Birtley, these shops are proving that local is best, and community trust is earned one order at a time.

Mealzo is proud to support and celebrate these outstanding partners, and we are excited to see what is next for each of them.

Congratulations once again to Chick Pizz, Get Stuffed, Ritz Fish & Chips and Sorrento Pizza, the pride of Newcastle’s takeaway scene.
