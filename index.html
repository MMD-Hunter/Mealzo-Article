Four Newcastle Takeaways Crowned Winners at Mealzo for Business Awards

Four independent takeaways across the Newcastle Upon Tyne area have been officially recognised at the Mealzo for Business Awards, taking home top honours in their respective categories.

The annual awards celebrate the best of local food businesses on the Mealzo platform — with winners selected based on consistent customer feedback, popular demand, and standout service.

Here’s a closer look at the champions of 2025:

Chick Pizz – Best Fried Chicken

Location: Denton Burn

Locals in Denton Burn already know Chick Pizz serves some of the best fried chicken in town — and now it’s official.

Stephen Phillip Burgon, owner of Chick Pizz, was thrilled to receive the Best Fried Chicken award. The shop’s signature golden, crispy chicken has become a favourite for customers across the area, with five-star reviews rolling in week after week.

“This win is all down to our amazing customers and our team who put the effort in every day. We’re proud to be part of the Mealzo community.”

Top seller: Fried chicken box – crisp, juicy, and full of flavour.

Get Stuffed – Best Kebab

Location: Newcastle upon Tyne

Get Stuffed has officially taken the title of Best Kebab in Newcastle — and customers will tell you it’s well-deserved.

Run by Kerim Seven, the busy city-centre takeaway has become known for generous portions, bold flavours, and freshly grilled kebabs that keep people coming back.

“We don’t cut corners – it’s quality ingredients, proper prep, and good service. Winning this means a lot because it’s based on what customers think.”

Top seller: Mixed doner kebab – stacked high and full of flavour.

Ritz Fish & Chips – Best Fish & Chips

Location: Columbia, Washington

Ritz Fish & Chips, based in Columbia, has been recognised for doing the classics right — winning Best Fish & Chips at the Mealzo awards.

Led by Manveer Singh, Ritz has earned a reputation for flaky cod, crisp batter, and perfectly cooked chips. With a focus on freshness and consistency, the shop has become a local staple.

“We’re all about keeping it simple and doing it well. This award gives us a real boost and we’re grateful to our customers for their support.”

Top seller: Traditional fish supper – cod, chips, and mushy peas.

Sorrento Pizza Ltd – Best Pizza

Location: Birtley

Sorrento Pizza Ltd, based in Birtley, has been crowned Best Pizza, topping the charts with their handcrafted pies and loyal following.

Owned by Ali Rowhani, the team at Sorrento pride themselves on fresh ingredients, fast service, and getting the details right.

“It means a lot to be recognised like this. We’ve worked hard to build something people love, and Mealzo has helped us reach more of them.”

Top seller: Classic pepperoni – rich tomato, bubbling mozzarella, and just the right kick.

Celebrating Local Excellence

These wins are a celebration of the hard work, passion, and dedication that go into running a successful independent food business. From Denton Burn to Birtley, the Mealzo platform is proud to support and showcase the best local talent in takeaway dining.

Congratulations to all four winners – the pride of Newcastle’s food scene.
