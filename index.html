Four Newcastle Takeaways Crowned Winners

The annual Mealzo for Business Awards celebrate the best of local food businesses, with winners selected based on consistent customer feedback, popular demand, and standout service.

Chicken

Chick Pizz – Best Fried Chicken

Denton Burn

Golden, crispy chicken that's become a local favourite.

Locals in Denton Burn already know Chick Pizz serves some of the best fried chicken in town—and now it's official.

Stephen Phillip Burgon, owner of Chick Pizz, was thrilled to receive the Best Fried Chicken award.

This win is all down to our amazing customers and our team who put the effort in every day. We're proud to be part of the Mealzo community.
Top Seller: Fried chicken box – crisp, juicy, and full of flavour
Kebab

Get Stuffed – Best Kebab

Newcastle upon Tyne

Generous portions and bold flavours in the city centre.

Get Stuffed has officially taken the title of Best Kebab in Newcastle—and customers will tell you it's well-deserved.

Run by Kerim Seven, the busy city-centre takeaway has become known for generous portions and bold flavours.

We don't cut corners – it's quality ingredients, proper prep, and good service. Winning this means a lot because it's based on what customers think.
Top Seller: Mixed doner kebab – stacked high and full of flavour
Fish

Ritz Fish & Chips – Best Fish & Chips

Columbia, Washington

Classic fish and chips done right with fresh ingredients.

Ritz Fish & Chips, based in Columbia, has been recognised for doing the classics right—winning Best Fish & Chips.

Led by Manveer Singh, Ritz has earned a reputation for flaky cod, crisp batter, and perfectly cooked chips.

We're all about keeping it simple and doing it well. This award gives us a real boost and we're grateful to our customers for their support.
Top Seller: Traditional fish supper – cod, chips, and mushy peas
Pizza

Sorrento Pizza Ltd – Best Pizza

Birtley

Handcrafted pizzas with fresh ingredients and fast service.

Sorrento Pizza Ltd, based in Birtley, has been crowned Best Pizza, topping the charts with their handcrafted pies.

Owned by Ali Rowhani, the team at Sorrento pride themselves on fresh ingredients and fast service.

It means a lot to be recognised like this. We've worked hard to build something people love, and Mealzo has helped us reach more of them.
Top Seller: Classic pepperoni – rich tomato, bubbling mozzarella, and just the right kick

Celebrating Local Excellence

These wins are a celebration of the hard work, passion, and dedication that go into running a successful independent food business. From Denton Burn to Birtley, the Mealzo platform is proud to support and showcase the best local talent in takeaway dining.

Congratulations to all four winners – the pride of Newcastle's food scene!
