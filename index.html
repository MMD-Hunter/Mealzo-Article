Chick Pizz – Best Fried Chicken

Denton Burn

Tucked away in the heart of Denton Burn, Chick Pizz has been quietly building a reputation as the area’s go-to spot for proper fried chicken. Run by Stephen Phillip Burgon, Chick Pizz is known for doing things the old-fashioned way. Real ingredients, careful preparation and a team that puts pride into every single box that leaves the shop.

“This win is all down to our amazing customers and our team who put the effort in every day. We are proud to be part of the Mealzo community, and we are absolutely buzzing with the recognition.”

Top seller: Fried chicken box

Get Stuffed – Best Kebab

Newcastle upon Tyne

In the heart of the city, Get Stuffed has built a loyal following, one flavour-packed wrap at a time. Owned and operated by Kerim Seven, the takeaway has earned a reputation for generous portions, freshly prepared ingredients and that hard-to-find balance of quality and speed.

“We do not cut corners. It is quality ingredients, proper prep and good service. Winning this means a lot because it is based on what customers think. That is what really matters to us.”

Top seller: Mixed doner kebab

Ritz Fish & Chips – Best Fish & Chips

Columbia, Washington

No British awards list would be complete without a proper chippy. Ritz Fish & Chips, owned by Manveer Singh, has proven that tradition, quality and consistency never go out of style. Their best-selling order is the classic fish supper: flaky cod, hand-cut chips and mushy peas served just as they should be.

“We are all about keeping it simple and doing it well. This award gives us a real boost and we are grateful to our customers for their support and trust over the years.”

Top seller: Traditional fish supper

Sorrento Pizza – Best Pizza

Birtley

Over in Birtley, Sorrento Pizza has been quietly leading the way. Founded by Ali Rowhani, Sorrento Pizza is built on one core principle: keep it fresh, and keep it real. That means handmade dough, high-quality toppings and pizzas cooked to perfection in their signature ovens.

“It means a lot to be recognised like this. We have worked hard to build something people love, and Mealzo has helped us reach more of them, especially those who might not have known we were just around the corner.”

Top seller: Classic pepperoni
