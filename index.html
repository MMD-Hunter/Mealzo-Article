Four Newcastle Takeaways Recognised

The annual Mealzo for Business Awards celebrate the best of local food businesses, with winners selected based on consistent customer feedback, popular demand, and standout service.

Chick Pizz – Best Fried Chicken

Denton Burn
Golden, crisp chicken – a Denton Burn favourite

Locals in Denton Burn have long favoured Chick Pizz for its exceptional fried chicken—and now the recognition is official.

Stephen Phillip Burgon, proprietor of Chick Pizz, expressed his gratitude for the Best Fried Chicken accolade.

This recognition is entirely down to our dedicated customers and hardworking team. We’re proud to be part of the Mealzo community.
Top seller: Fried chicken box – crisp, juicy, and full of flavour

Get Stuffed – Best Kebab

Newcastle upon Tyne
Generous portions, bold flavours – city centre favourite

Get Stuffed has officially been recognised as the home of the finest kebab in Newcastle—a distinction well deserved according to its loyal clientele.

Managed by Kerim Seven, this city-centre establishment is renowned for generous portions and bold, distinctive flavours.

We never cut corners—only the finest ingredients, careful preparation, and quality service. This award means a lot as it’s driven by what our customers truly favour.
Top seller: Mixed doner kebab – stacked high and brimming with flavour

Ritz Fish & Chips – Best Fish & Chips

Columbia, Washington
Classic fish and chips, freshly made – always popular

Ritz Fish & Chips, based in Columbia, has been recognised for excellence in traditional fare—earning the Best Fish & Chips title.

Under the stewardship of Manveer Singh, Ritz is celebrated for its flaky cod, crisp batter, and properly cooked chips.

Our ethos is to keep it simple and do it well. This recognition truly motivates us and we’re grateful for the continued support of our customers.
Top seller: Traditional fish supper – cod, chips, and mushy peas

Sorrento Pizza Ltd – Best Pizza

Birtley
Handcrafted pizzas, fresh ingredients, fast service

Sorrento Pizza Ltd, located in Birtley, has topped the rankings as Best Pizza, praised for handcrafted quality.

Owner Ali Rowhani and his team focus on using fresh ingredients and prompt service to delight customers.

It means a great deal to be recognised in this way. We’ve worked hard to create something people in our community truly favour, and Mealzo has helped us reach even more of them.
Top seller: Classic pepperoni – rich tomato, bubbling mozzarella, and just the right kick

Celebrating Local Excellence

These awards honour the dedication, skill, and passion behind every independent food business. From Denton Burn to Birtley, Mealzo proudly champions the finest local talent in the North East’s takeaway scene.

Congratulations to all four recipients—the pride of Newcastle’s food community.
