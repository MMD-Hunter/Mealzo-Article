Joe’s – Hidden Gem Award

East Lothian

Joe’s, a family-run takeaway nestled in East Lothian, has taken home the Hidden Gem Award at this year’s Mealzo for Business Awards. It is a fitting recognition for a shop that has steadily built a reputation through word of mouth and local support.

Led by Dario Andrucci, Joe’s has always focused on quality ingredients, traditional recipes and genuine service. Though it may not sit on a high street or shout loudly online, its loyal following and glowing reviews show that good food speaks for itself.

Customer favourites include freshly fried fish, handmade pizzas and kebabs. Each order reflects the passion that goes into running a proper local business.

“It’s an honour to be called a hidden gem. We’re thankful to Mealzo and especially to our customers who continue to support us. This award is for them.”

With the help of Mealzo, Joe’s has quietly grown into a local favourite beyond its immediate area, connecting with new customers while keeping its charm intact.

Top seller: Handmade pizzas
