Lothian Takeaways Honoured at the Mealzo for Business Awards

Outstanding local takeaways across West and East Lothian have been celebrated at this year’s Mealzo for Business Awards, taking home honours in categories that reflect their popularity, service and quality.

Chosen based on glowing customer reviews, food excellence and their growth through the Mealzo platform, these award-winning businesses represent the heart of local food culture. From indulgent desserts to classic chippies and hidden gems, the 2025 winners are proof that great taste is never far away in the Lothians.

Here is a closer look at the shops making a name for themselves this year:
Bene’s – Best Takeaway
East Lothian
In East Lothian, one name continues to stand out on the takeaway scene. Bene’s has officially taken home the title of Best Takeaway, a recognition earned through consistently positive customer feedback and a dedication to food done right.

Owned by Hussain Omer, Bene’s is a local institution that offers a bit of everything, but with care and attention that sets them apart. Their bestsellers include traditional fish and chips, hearty burgers and pizzas that keep customers coming back.

Whether it is a family tea or a quick bite after work, Bene’s has become a trusted name in the community. Reviewers praise the food quality, generous portions and friendly staff.
“Thanks to Mealzo for helping us grow our customer base and be seen by more people. We really appreciate the support and are proud of this achievement.”
Mealzo’s platform has helped Bene’s expand its reach while staying true to its roots. The award recognises their consistency and popularity with customers throughout East Lothian.
Joe’s – Hidden Gem Award
East Lothian
Joe’s, a family-run takeaway nestled in East Lothian, has taken home the Hidden Gem Award at this year’s Mealzo for Business Awards. It is a fitting recognition for a shop that has steadily built a reputation through word of mouth and local support.

Led by Dario Andrucci, Joe’s has always focused on quality ingredients, traditional recipes and genuine service. Though it may not sit on a high street or shout loudly online, its loyal following and glowing reviews show that good food speaks for itself.

Customer favourites include freshly fried fish, handmade pizzas and kebabs. Each order reflects the passion that goes into running a proper local business.
“It’s an honour to be called a hidden gem. We’re thankful to Mealzo and especially to our customers who continue to support us. This award is for them.”
With the help of Mealzo, Joe’s has quietly grown into a local favourite beyond its immediate area, connecting with new customers while keeping its charm intact.
TKH Fish Bar – Customer Favourite
East Lothian
Known for its crisp batter, golden chips and generous portions, TKH Fish Bar has officially won the Customer Favourite award in East Lothian. The title reflects the overwhelming support from regulars who keep coming back for one reason – consistently great food.

Owner Sabir Eroglu puts customer experience at the heart of everything. From the moment an order is placed to the final bite, the team focuses on freshness, quality and speed.

TKH’s signature dish, the classic fish supper, remains their most popular. But the menu also includes kebabs, burgers and pizzas that cater to every craving.
“We’re grateful for this recognition and we want to thank Mealzo and every customer who’s taken the time to leave a review or place an order. It keeps us going.”
TKH Fish Bar has seen steady growth through its partnership with Mealzo, gaining visibility across East Lothian and earning praise for both flavour and service.

Local Talent on the Rise

These Lothian takeaways are more than just popular – they are part of the community, trusted by locals and supported by loyal customers. The Mealzo for Business Awards shine a light on their hard work and commitment to quality, celebrating the unique flavour each shop brings to the region.

Congratulations to Bene’s, Joe’s and TKH Fish Bar for their well-earned wins.
