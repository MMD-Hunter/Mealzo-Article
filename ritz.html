Ritz Fish & Chips – Best Fish & Chips

Columbia, Washington

No British awards list would be complete without a proper chippy. Ritz Fish & Chips, owned by Manveer Singh, has proven that tradition, quality and consistency never go out of style. Their best-selling order is the classic fish supper: flaky cod, hand-cut chips and mushy peas served just as they should be.

“We are all about keeping it simple and doing it well. This award gives us a real boost and we are grateful to our customers for their support and trust over the years.”

Top seller: Traditional fish supper
