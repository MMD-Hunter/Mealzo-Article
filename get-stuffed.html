Get Stuffed – Best Kebab

Newcastle upon Tyne

In the heart of the city, Get Stuffed has built a loyal following, one flavour-packed wrap at a time. Owned and operated by Kerim Seven, the takeaway has earned a reputation for generous portions, freshly prepared ingredients and that hard-to-find balance of quality and speed.

“We do not cut corners. It is quality ingredients, proper prep and good service. Winning this means a lot because it is based on what customers think. That is what really matters to us.”
