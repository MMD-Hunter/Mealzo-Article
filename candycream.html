Candy Cream – Best Dessert

West Lothian

When it comes to sweet treats, Candy Cream has quickly become the go-to dessert destination in West Lothian. Now crowned winner of the Best Dessert category, the team at Candy Cream have proven that quality, variety and customer service make all the difference.

Owned by Imran Iqbal, Candy Cream offers everything from indulgent cookie dough to freshly made waffles, ice cream shakes and more. Each item is made to order, with attention to presentation and flavour that keeps customers raving.

Their reviews speak for themselves, with loyal customers highlighting both the desserts and the service. Whether it is a weekend treat or a celebration with friends, Candy Cream delivers on taste every time.

Imran said: “We are so thankful to Mealzo and our amazing customers. This award means a lot to our whole team. It’s a big motivation to keep doing what we love.”

The partnership with Mealzo has allowed Candy Cream to reach more dessert lovers across the region, with online orders climbing steadily throughout the year.
