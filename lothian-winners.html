Lothian Takeaways Honoured at the Mealzo for Business Awards

Outstanding local takeaways across West and East Lothian have been celebrated at this year’s Mealzo for Business Awards, taking home honours in categories that reflect their popularity, service and quality.

Chosen based on glowing customer reviews, food excellence and their growth through the Mealzo platform, these award-winning businesses represent the heart of local food culture. From indulgent desserts to classic chippies and hidden gems, the 2025 winners are proof that great taste is never far away in the Lothians.
