Bathgate Bitez – Best Newcomer

West Lothian

Bathgate Bitez burst onto the scene this year and quickly became a local favourite. Focusing on gourmet burgers with locally sourced ingredients, owner David Chen has created a menu that's both inventive and satisfying.

“We're absolutely thrilled to win Best Newcomer. It's been a whirlwind opening year, and the support from the community and Mealzo has been incredible. This is for our amazing team and loyal customers.”

This award recognises their immediate impact, glowing customer reviews, and dedication to raising the bar for takeaway food in the area.

Top seller: The 'Bite-sized Beast' Burger
